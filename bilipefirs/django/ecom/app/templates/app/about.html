Our Story

Founded by Henil Shingala, Luxury Supercars is the result of passion, dedication, and innovation. As a diploma student in Computer Science Engineering, I built this full-stack e-commerce website using Django entirely on my own. Over 10 months, I brought this vision to life, combining technology with the world of luxury automobiles.

This platform is not just about cars—it's about bringing the ultimate supercar experience to enthusiasts worldwide.
